1. Intelligent Preset Generator
Create presets by discovering device parameters, then intelligently populate them based on desired characteristics (e.g., "bright bass," "aggressive pad," "smooth reverb"). The system learns parameter ranges and constraints, then generates variations that stay musically useful instead of random.

2. Real-Time Device State Versioning & Undo
Snapshot every device state on your tracks, then rollback to any previous state. More powerful than Ableton's native undo because you can jump between specific device configurations across multiple tracks simultaneously.

3. Parameter Automation Painter
Use M4L to discover all hidden parameters, then paint complex automation curves across multiple parameters at once—like drawing a "filter sweep + resonance + saturation ramp" in one gesture. Creates sounds that evolve naturally.

4. VST/AU Parameter Mapper
Scan all your third-party plugins, discover their hidden parameters, and create custom control surfaces that map them to simpler names. Build your own mixing console that exposes only what you care about.

5. Adaptive Mixing Assistant
Analyze track devices, discover compressor parameters, EQ curves, saturation—then auto-generate mixing moves. "Make this track brighter" → automatically adjusts EQ cutoff, saturation reduction, and delay on all devices in the chain.

6. Generative Automation Sequencer
Use discovered parameters to create step-based automation that locks to your BPM. Each step modulates hidden parameters on synths/effects in rhythmic patterns (filter sweeps, resonance pulses, compression breathing, etc.).

7. Device Chain Explorer & Visualizer
Map out your entire device hierarchy, show all connected parameters and their ranges, then visually represent how changes cascade through the chain. Great for understanding complex setups.

8. Cross-Track Parameter Linking
Link parameters across different tracks' devices (e.g., "Synth filter cutoff moves with Bassline filter cutoff" or "Reverb wet increases when drums Saturate increases"). Create cohesive sonic evolution.

9. Smart Macro Controller
Discover all devices on a track, identify their most "interesting" hidden parameters, then automatically map them to a master macro knob. No manual setup—just "create a super-knob" and it figures out what to control.

10. Preset Morph Engine
Load two completely different device presets, then smoothly morph between them over N bars/beats by interpolating all parameters (including hidden ones). Creates seamless transitions between drastically different sounds.

11. Parameter Randomizer with Constraints
Randomize any device's parameters while respecting musicality constraints (e.g., "never go below 40Hz on filters," "keep attack under 50ms," "resonance stays 0-80"). Generate variations that always sound intentional.

12. Live Parameter Capture & Playback
Record your manual knob movements in real-time (even hidden parameters), then loop them or apply them to other devices. Turn live performance into reusable automation data.

13. Device Swap Preserver
When you swap devices, automatically detect parameter similarity and transfer automation/settings to the new device. "Replaced Wavetable with Serum? Here's all your automation remapped."

14. Conditional Automation Sequences
Create branching automation logic: "If kick hits hard (audio analysis), then compress this track harder" or "If filter is above 8kHz, reduce saturation." Reactive parameter movement.

15. Parameter Dependency Mapper
Discover which hidden parameters affect each other, then create "linked" control schemes. E.g., "Wavetable oscillator mix changes automatically when unison spread changes" to maintain tone balance.
